Vanlife Benefits

  1. Absolute Freedom - You can go anywhere you want and wake up to a new view everyday
  2. More at one with the World - Being able to stop in the most remote destinations, out of the city life hustle, connecting more with nature
  3. Being part of something great - The van life community is an all insiring, friendly and welcoming family of adventure
  4. Minimalism - Letting go of unnecessary clutter and learning to live with the bare essentials
  5. Eco-friendly - Living in a van, helps you think about things like reducing waste because you have such limited resources, in turn helping lower your carbon footprint

Vanlife Hacks

  1. Build your trips up - You do not have to jump right in with the biggest van adventure trip. Start off with smaller trips to get used to vanlife and build yourself up
  2. Plan your overnight stays - If you can, look to plan your overnight stays ahead of your journey, so you can benefit from free campsites or wild camping spots
  3. Simple is better - Being on the road with a limited size cooking space, its best to aim for simple meals that can be cooked in small & limited spaces
  4. Stealth Camping - In those unforseen circumstances where you cannot find a place to stay, in an urban area or city, you may be able to go under the rader by parking up in a place late at night and leaving real early to go by undetected
  5. Let you home on wheels breath - wherever possible, depending on the weather, try to keep a window open, even just slightly. This will help with the condensation build up
